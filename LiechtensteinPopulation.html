Description of Liechtenstein's Population Trend

The population of this small country is growing at an astounding exponential rate. Primarily due to it's amazing healthcare and wealthy population.

Liechtenstein's History versus Population

There were no significant events that altered the population of Liechtenstein. This was due to them not participating in WWI and remaining neutral in WWII. However due to the wealth of the small country they have a very steady growth rate, causing a steady increase in population.
